ALU

The Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU) is a key part of the CPU, handling arithmetic and logical operations like addition, subtraction, and comparisons. This project designs a combinational module to simulate a basic ALU function. It takes two 4-bit inputs, A and B, and performs addition or subtraction based on the control signal M0. The result, F, is limited to 4 bits (max value 9) to prevent carry/borrow issues. This simplified design offers insight into ALU functionality and its role in processor operations.
